Portfolio overview, objectives and structure

[ SECTION 1 — INTRODUCTION ]
Toyota Gazoo Racing (TGR) is the global motorsport and high-performance division of Toyota Motor Corporation. The brand plays a central role in Toyota’s performance positioning, competing in elite competitions such as the 24 Hours of Le Mans and the World Rally Championship. Beyond racing, TGR contributes to the development of performance-oriented road cars such as the GR Yaris and GR Supra. In the digital era, social media platforms particularly Instagram have become essential tools for automotive brands to engage audiences. Instagram’s visual-first interface makes it particularly suitable for motorsport brands, enabling them to communicate speed, design, and excitement effectively. This report evaluates how Toyota Gazoo Racing uses Instagram to engage consumers through the Ladder of Engagement framework (Chaffey and Smith, 2022) and critically assesses the platform using digital marketing theory. The analysis demonstrates how TGR transforms passive viewers into active brand advocates while identifying areas for improvement.
